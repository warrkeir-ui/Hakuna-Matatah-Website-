Classrooms

Each of our classrooms provides a developmentally appropriate learning environment to foster your child's natural desire to explore and learn.

Simba (Infants)

Curriculum

Our infant curriculum focuses on a child's individual needs and developmental milestones through exploring and playtime. We use assessments to create a unique path for each child, supporting early growth in a caring and responsive way.

Daily Schedule

  • 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Drop-off & Free Exploration
  • 9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Morning Snack & Diapering
  • 9:30 AM - 10:30 AM: Gross Motor Play & Circle Time
  • 10:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Tummy Time & Sensory Play
  • 11:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Lunch
  • 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Naptime
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Diapering & Afternoon Snack
  • 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Outdoor Exploration
  • 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Quiet Activities & Reading
  • 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Pick-up
